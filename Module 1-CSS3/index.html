📰 Community Bulletin

Welcome to the July edition of our Community Bulletin! This month marks a milestone — our portal has connected over 5,000 residents across 12 neighbourhoods. From park cleanups to tech workshops, the spirit of community has never been stronger.


The Parks & Recreation department reminds residents that Riverside Trail will be partially closed from June 28 to July 3 for resurfacing. Cyclists and joggers are advised to use the alternate Northern Loop during this period.


The Library is expanding its digital literacy program for seniors. Volunteer tutors are needed every Tuesday evening. If you have basic computer skills and a passion for helping others, please sign up at the front desk or register online through this portal. Refreshments are provided.


Construction on the new Community Hub is on schedule. The facility will feature a 200-seat auditorium, three multi-purpose halls, a rooftop garden, and co-working spaces for local entrepreneurs. Completion is expected by October 2025.
